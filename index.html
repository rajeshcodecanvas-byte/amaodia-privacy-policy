Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 24, 2024

Welcome to AmaOdia. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any data processed by our application. This policy outlines our practices for the AmaOdia Android App.

Transparency Note: AmaOdia is designed to function primarily offline. We do not collect, upload, or sell your personal data to any third-party servers.

1. Information Handling (Local Storage)

The App stores information locally on your device to provide a personalized experience. This data includes:

  • Search History: Saved locally via a Room database to allow you to revisit recent translations.
  • Favorites: Words you choose to save for quick access are kept on your phone's internal storage.

Note: This data is deleted automatically if you uninstall the application.

2. Mandatory Permissions & Their Usage

  • Internet & Network State: Used to fetch dictionary updates, pronunciations, or translation data from our APIs. No personally identifiable information is transmitted.
  • Clipboard Access: Our Floating Widget monitors the system clipboard to detect copied words for instant translation. This is processed entirely on-device.
  • Overlay Permission (Display Over Other Apps): Allows the App to show the translation bubble while you are using other applications.
  • Notification Permission: Required to deliver the "Word of the Day" updates and service status notifications.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party SDKs that may collect anonymized technical data (like device model or crash logs) to improve app stability:

  • Google Play Services (Updates & Licensing)
  • Google Text-to-Speech (Pronunciation)

4. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and code obfuscation (R8/ProGuard) to protect the App’s internal logic. All local data is stored in the app's private sandbox, inaccessible to other applications.

5. Children’s Privacy

AmaOdia does not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13. Since no user accounts are required, we do not track user age.
